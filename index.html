How to follow UV index properly

  • 0-2 (Low): Safe to be outside.
  • 3-5 (Moderate): Shade, sunscreen, sunglasses.
  • 6-7 (High): Protective clothing and sunscreen.
  • 8-10 (Very High): Avoid sun during midday.
  • 11+ (Extreme): Take all precautions.
